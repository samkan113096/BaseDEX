**Day 54**
> Thread: The BaseDEX architecture explained 🧵
>
> How does a perp DEX actually work? Thread time.
>
> [1/5] You sign a limit/market order with your wallet. This creates an EIP-712 message — cryptographically binding the exact trade terms to your wallet.

---

**Day 55**
> [2/5] Our off-chain matching engine receives your order.
>
> It's a price-time priority order book — identical to what exchanges like Coinbase Pro use, but decentralized.
>
> Best price + earliest timestamp = fills first. Simple. Fair.

---

**Day 56**
> [3/5] When two orders match, our engine generates a settlement payload.
>
> [4/5] That payload is submitted on-chain to SpotEngine.sol or PerpEngine.sol.
>
> [5/5] The contract verifies both signatures, moves collateral, updates positions.
>
> Result: CEX speed, DEX custody. The best of both worlds.
